Pro Tips

How to get crispy waffles?

+

Don't open the waffle iron too early! Wait until steam stops coming out and the waffle is golden brown.

Can I make the batter ahead of time?

+

Yes! You can make batter up to 2 hours ahead. Just give it a gentle stir before using.

What toppings work best?

+

Fresh berries, maple syrup, whipped cream, chocolate chips, or even savory options like bacon and cheese!

Waffle Variations

Try these delicious variations to spice up your waffle game!

Chocolate Chip

Add 1/2 cup chocolate chips to your batter for an indulgent treat!

Blueberry Bliss

Fold in fresh blueberries for a burst of fruity flavor in every bite.

Cinnamon Spice

Add 1 tsp cinnamon and a pinch of nutmeg for warm, cozy flavors.

Banana Nut

Mash a ripe banana into the batter and add chopped walnuts.
