What ingredients do I need to make waffles?

+

You'll need flour, eggs, milk, baking powder, sugar, butter, and a pinch of salt. Mix them together and you're good to go!

How long does it take to make homemade waffles?

+

From start to finish, it takes about 15-20 minutes! Mix the batter (5 mins), heat the waffle iron (3-5 mins), and cook each waffle (2-3 mins).
